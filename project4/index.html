About Me

My name is Jalen Terrell Lane. I am 19 years old, and I was born on August 26th 2004. I've lived in a few places, but I currently live in Maryland.
My goal in life is to find something that I enjoy so much that I don't want to retire. I believe that if you truly enjoy what you do, there is no such thing as retirement.
The reason that I chose to enter the web development field is because it combines two things I enjoy doing: designing things and programming! Becoming a front-end developer allows me to design websites as well as create cool features for them.

Background

As a military child, I moved frequently. I've been to a total of 5 schools, and I finished high school in Maryland. After high school, I went to Penn State for a year. My first year didn't go very well, so I decided to move to online school because I would be closer to home, and it was cheaper.

Future Goals

The end goal is for me to have my own business where clients hire me to create websites and graphics for their businesses. The reason I want to become self-employed is because I want to be able to control my work hours, my rates, and what I will and will not do. I believe that everyone should try to be self-employed in some capacity, or have a side hustle that they can earn a secondary income source from.
